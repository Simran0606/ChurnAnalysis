## Power BI Dashboard

Alongside the machine learning model, a Power BI dashboard was created to visualise customer churn patterns and support business decision-making.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

- Overall customer churn rate
- Number of churned, stayed and joined customers
- Churn distribution by customer demographics
- Churn by contract type, payment method and internet service
- Revenue impact of churned customers
- Customer tenure and monthly charge patterns
- Key churn reasons and churn categories
- High-risk customer segments for retention focus

The dashboard was designed to make the model insights more accessible to business users and support proactive churn reduction strategies.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The main objectives of the Power BI dashboard were to:

- Provide a clear summary of customer churn performance
- Identify customer segments with higher churn risk
- Highlight service, contract and billing factors linked to churn
- Support retention planning using data-driven insights
- Present findings in a simple and interactive format for stakeholders

---

## Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes:

- KPI cards for total customers, churned customers, churn rate and revenue
- Bar charts showing churn by contract type, state and payment method
- Donut or pie charts showing customer status distribution
- Trend or distribution visuals for tenure, monthly charges and revenue
- Churn category and churn reason analysis
- Interactive filters for customer segment exploration
